Frequently Asked Questions

What is a text message AI assistant?

A text message AI assistant lets you send requests in plain language, like a text or SMS, and get help without opening a separate app. With Super, those messages can trigger real computer actions, not just replies.

How is Super different from Siri, ChatGPT, Gemini, or Grok by text?

Siri, ChatGPT, Gemini, and Grok are strong conversational assistants. Super is designed for durable workflows: it can operate a real computer on your behalf and reuse past work through a computer-use cache so repeated tasks get faster and cheaper over time.

What is a computer-use cache?

A computer-use cache is Super’s record of how a task was completed on a computer before. When you ask for the same or similar task again, Super can reuse that cached work instead of starting from scratch.

Can Super make purchases or bookings automatically?

Super can assist with purchase or booking workflows by operating a computer and preparing the steps, but it is designed to require user review and confirmation where appropriate.
